


🚀 PropClose AI: Strategic Blueprint &amp; Vision Document

1. Executive Summary
PropClose AI is an AI-first Sales Automation Engine designed for Real Estate Brokers. Unlike passive CRMs, it acts as an Active AI Salesman that captures leads in real-time, initiates conversations within 30 seconds, handles objections using project-specific knowledge (RAG), and schedules site visits.

2. Core Problem Statement

Lead Decay: Brokers fail to respond within the critical 5-minute window.

Operational Overhead: Manually sharing brochures and coordinating site visits is inefficient.

Follow-up Fatigue: Brokers either forget to follow up or do it inconsistently, leading to lost revenue.

3. Advanced Product Pillars

A. Data &amp; RAG Foundation

Strict Property Mapping: Uses normalized exact matching to prevent data leaks between projects.

Knowledge Retrieval: Processes brochures into vector embeddings to provide expert-level answers.

B. High-Fidelity Lead Ingestion (Option B)

Omnichannel: Real-time ingestion from Meta, Google, and Portals.

Mandatory Phone Validation: Every lead must have a validated phone number for CRM reliability.

Lead Deduplication: Prevents multiple entries for the same user per project.

C. Scalable Multi-Tenant Architecture

High Concurrency: Designed to support hundreds of brokers simultaneously without latency.

Fast Response: Utilizes asynchronous processing (FastAPI + BackgroundTasks) to ensure the system remains responsive even under heavy load.

4. Intelligent Follow-up &amp; Anti-Spam Logic
To ensure high conversion without getting the WhatsApp number blocked:

Human-Centric Follow-ups: Instead of "Buy Now" spam, the tool uses "Value-Add" follow-ups (e.g., sharing a new construction update video or a limited-time offer).

Smart Frequency Capping: AI tracks user engagement. If a user doesn't reply, follow-up frequency decreases automatically to avoid irritation.

Spam Protection: Implements randomized delays and natural language variations to mimic human typing behavior, protecting the WhatsApp Business API account from being flagged as a bot.

Opt-out Handling: Immediate cessation of messages if a customer indicates they are not interested.

5. The Sales Funnel (The "Hook")

Immediate Response: Automated greeting within 30 seconds of lead capture.

Nurturing: AI answers questions about carpet area, pricing, and amenities using the RAG engine.

The Closing: The primary KPI is Site Visit Booking, integrating with Google/Outlook calendars for both broker and client.

6. Technical &amp; UI Principles

Tech Stack: FastAPI (Backend), Next.js (Frontend), Supabase (Database/Vector Store).

UI/UX: Modern, mobile-first dashboard using Shadcn/UI and Tailwind CSS for brokers on the move.

Reliability: Defensive coding with Pydantic validation and comprehensive DB indexing for speed.
